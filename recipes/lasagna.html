Lasagna

Description

Mouth watering classic lasagna dish

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. pasta
  • 500 ml pasta sauce
  • 1 lb. italian sausage
  • 1 lb. mushrooms
  • 1 lb. cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water and add pasta
  2. Cook sausage
  3. Cook mushrooms
  4. Mix cooked mushrooms and sausage in the pasta sauce
  5. Layer cooked pasta with sauce mix
  6. Add cheese
  7. Bake on 425 degrees for 45 mins
